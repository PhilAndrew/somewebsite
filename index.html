About Us

Company Profile

HongKong Rainbow-Bridge Technology Company Limited (hereafter HOQTEC) is an innovative high-tech SME that was founded in September 2014. Our team was originally formed from Eucalyptus company (U.S.A), Actions Company (NASDAQ: ACTS), Dendrite company, Gilbarco Veeder-Root Company, Australia NSW railway ticket bookings system and other well known companies. Our team specializes in IT information systems, cloud computing and cloud services in areas with strong technical accumulation, and has a wide range of products. We work with many partners, mainly engaged in cloud computing, cloud services (IAAS, PAAS) technology development and the provision of related technical services. We carry out cloud computing (such as OpenStack, Softlayer and Storage) and associated cutting-edge technology research and also development in Hong Kong, marketing and technical services.

Our Products

Product Description

  1. MCP (cloudy Management Platform)

    OpenStack interface (ui) on the basis of self-developed management platform cloudy. Customers can be unified in a private cloud environment on a variety of its public cloud resources (compute, storage and network). Customers do not need to switch between different public and private clouds, such as different management interface, centralized management of various cloud platforms.

  2. Cloud Storage

    Hybrid cloud storage. Customers in the local private cloud storage, while backing up data, cloud storage gateway system (gateway) automatically or according to customer-defined strategies to store, back up the appropriate data to the public cloud, customers throughout the process without intervention. Customers will receive multiple copies of the data, when any part of the data loss can be automatically restored by replicating data.

  3. IBM cloud computing product sales agent, Mirantis OpenStack product

    Sales, cloud computing design consulting services and implementation and after-sales service and training services OpenStack
